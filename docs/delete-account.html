Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you'd like to delete your SubTracker account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your subscriptions, trials, and settings will be permanently removed.

What gets deleted:

  • Your account and login credentials
  • All subscription records you've added
  • All trial tracking data
  • Your app preferences and settings
  • Notification history

How to request deletion:

Send an email to the address below with the subject line "Delete My Account" and include the email address associated with your SubTracker account.

Request Account Deletion

We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.
