Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 26, 2025

SubTracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address - Used for account authentication and communication
  • Display name - If you sign in with Google, we receive your name from your Google profile

User-Generated Content

We store data you voluntarily provide:

  • Subscription details - Service names, costs, billing dates, and categories you enter
  • Trial information - Free trial tracking data you create
  • App preferences - Your currency selection and notification settings

Device Information

We may collect:

  • Device type and operating system version (for app compatibility)
  • Push notification tokens (to send reminders you've enabled)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the SubTracker service
  • To authenticate your account and secure your data
  • To send push notification reminders for trials and subscriptions (when enabled)
  • To sync your data across devices

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Supabase

We use Supabase as our backend service to store your account and subscription data securely. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.

Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive basic profile information (email and display name) from Google. We do not access your Google contacts, calendar, or other Google services. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Unavatar

We use Unavatar to display service logos. This service may receive domain names of services you track to fetch their logos.

Data Security

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text
  • Row-level security ensures you can only access your own data
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - View all data we have about you within the app
  • Correction - Edit your subscriptions and profile information
  • Deletion - Delete individual subscriptions/trials or your entire account
  • Data Portability - Request a copy of your data by contacting us

Children's Privacy

SubTracker is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
